Lawn Fertillization

Assist your grass for a vibrant & healthier lawn.

Fertilization is key to a healthy, verdant lawn, and our team at Unlimited Lawn Care will give it everything it needs to thrive all year long! We use a granular, slow-release fertilizer that'll gradually nourish your grass over an extended period; we'll apply these treatments a total of five times throughout the year to consistently supply your lawn with nutrients like nitrogen, potassium, and phosphorus. But wait, there's more - our lawn fertilization program also includes pre- and post-emergent weed control treatments to ensure weeds aren't stealing your turf's precious nutrients!

We offer our lawn fertilization service to residential properties in and around Denton, TX, including Corith and Ponder.

Lawn Mowing

Ensure you lawn is looking its best with lawn mowing.

Our lawn mowing service ensures your lawn stays well-manicured. We mow your lawn from March to November on a weekly basis to keep your grass neat and tidy. We also string-trim, blow grass clipping debris, edge, and follow best practices when performing our mowing service.

We rotate our mowing patterns at every visit and adjust the height of our mower deck according to your turf type.
